About BellCon

No need to leave your couch - this year's BellCon can be experienced from the comfort of your own home!

That's right! BellCon has gone totally digital this year to meet the needs of a rapidly changing world. You'll be able to see all your favorite speakers without having to change out of your pajamas!

Familiar Faces and New Friends

This year's speaker lineup includes an eclectic mix of noteworthy townspeople, fan favorites, and some new faces!

From new fashion trends to fishing tips and tricks, our speakers have something to offer for everyone!

Check out a sampling of this year's speakers below!

Highlights

Fishing: Find out how to land the rare ones! Housing: Learn how you can have the coolest home in town! Town planning: Get some tips on making your town beautiful! Museum: See what the latest exhibits have to offer! Turnips: Learn how to play the stalk market - and win! Fashion: Hear about the latest trends!

Speakers

Tom Nook

Come on, you know who this guy is! Everyone's favorite and most "generous" landlord, Tom is always willing to expand your home - for the right price!

Isabelle

Voted most valuable team player this year, Isabelle works tirelessly in her position as town administrator to make sure your town is the best it can be.

Mabel Able

As the bubbly face of the Able Sisters clothing store, fashion guru Mabel will leave no fabulous stone unturned until she helps you find the perfect look.

Blathers

Museum curator extraordinaire, Blathers is an enthusiast of everything that nature has to offer. Well - except for those disgusting bugs...

K.K. Slider

The coolest cat - err, dog - around, K.K. is known far and wide for his dulcet tones, masterful guitar skills, and eclectic blend of various musical styles.

C.J.

One of the newer faces in this year's lineup, C.J. is more than happy to share the hints and tricks he's picked up while becoming a fishing expert.
